Get Started

For small businesses

What you need

  • A board
  • Some paper
  • Logo (download)

It's as simple as that.

Just display some "tickets" or "tokens" or whatever you can on an accessible display board in your shop for customers to come in and pick up to exchange for a particular item or service.

Whenever a paying customer donates towards the sharing and caring scheme, add another ticket to the board

Print off the sharing and caring logo and display it in your shop window to alert passers-by that you have signed up to the scheme.
